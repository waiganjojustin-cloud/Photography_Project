Biography

My journey toward understanding the concept of the "Matrix system" has been shaped by curiosity, critical thinking, and a desire to understand how the world operates beneath the surface.
Rather than accepting information at face value, I have developed a habit of asking questions, exploring different perspectives, and examining the systems that influence our daily lives.
To me, the Matrix system represents the interconnected structures that shape society, including education, technology, economics, culture, and human behavior.
Studying these relationships has encouraged me to think independently, challenge assumptions, and appreciate the complexity of the world around me.
